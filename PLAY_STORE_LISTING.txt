App name: CNC & Me
Subtitle: Machining Calculator & Chip Load Tool
Short description:
Dial in chip load, feeds, and cutting strategy with a visual hmax map.

Full description:
CNC & Me is a machining calculator built for real-world cutting decisions.

Instead of just numbers, it shows you where your cut sits relative to the correct chip thickness range and how to fix it.

Features:
- Chip load and feed calculator
- Visual hmax position map
- Standard endmill and high feed modes
- Dynamic feed tuning with live preview
- Clear guidance on what to adjust and why
- Fully offline, no login required

Designed for machinists who want fast, reliable answers on the shop floor.

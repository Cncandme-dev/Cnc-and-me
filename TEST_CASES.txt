CNC & Me quick verification cases

These are checked against the same equations used in the app:
RPM = (1000 × Vc) / (pi × D)
Feed = RPM × z × fz
Endmill actual hmax = programmed fz × radial chip thinning factor
HFM actual hmax = programmed fz × sin(lead angle)
MRR = ae × ap × feed / 1000
Power = MRR × unit power

1) Endmill | 316 Stainless | Adaptive
Inputs:
D 16 mm
z 4
Vc 120 m/min
fz 0.10 mm/tooth
ae 2 mm
ap 12 mm
Expected:
RPM 2387
Feed 955 mm/min
Actual hmax 0.066 mm
Chip thinning factor 0.661
MRR 22.9 cm^3/min
Power 1.03 kW

2) Endmill | 316 Stainless | Slotting

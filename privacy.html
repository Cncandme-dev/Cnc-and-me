CNC & Me

Privacy policy

CNC & Me performs machining calculations locally on the device. This build does not send calculation inputs or results to a server, does not require account creation, and does not include advertising or analytics by default.

Data collection

No personal data is intentionally collected by this build. Any values you enter stay on your device unless you choose to share them yourself.

Permissions

This build does not request device permissions beyond standard browser storage used for offline caching.

Children

CNC & Me is a machining utility and is not directed to children.

Changes

If the app later adds cloud sync, analytics, or user accounts, this policy should be updated before release.
